## Tactical Rules

- **Never link directly in post body** — LinkedIn algorithm penalizes external links. Put links in the first comment.
- **Standard footer** on every post: who you are, what you do, how to reach you.
- **Tag/mention people** referenced in the post — pulls them into the discussion.
- **Engage actively** — comment on others' posts. LinkedIn rewards participation, not just publishing.
