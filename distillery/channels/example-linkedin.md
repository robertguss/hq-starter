## Strategy

Three posts per week, rotating themes:

1. **Technical** — your core expertise, tooling, workflows
2. **Personal journey** — founder path, lessons learned, team building
3. **Case studies** — client projects, challenges, outcomes

## Anchor Keywords

Define 3-5 keywords your content orbits around. Everything you publish should connect to at least one.

## Voice & Format

- Define your signature opening (if any)
- Concise, concrete, no digressions. Say what you mean, build narrative around one point.
- Conversational but professional. Humor welcome, not forced.

## Tactical Rules

- **Never link directly in post body** — LinkedIn algorithm penalizes external links. Put links in the first comment.
- **Standard footer** on every post: who you are, what you do, how to reach you.
- **Tag/mention people** — pull others into discussion, especially people referenced in the post
- **Engage actively** — comment and react on others' posts. LinkedIn rewards participation, not just publishing.

## The Ring (review panel)

After every draft, run the post through these reviewers. Each grades 0-10 with specific notes. Iterate until combined score is 8+ and you approve.

| Reviewer                  | Perspective                                           |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Copywriter**            | Writing quality, structure, strongest/weakest lines   |
| **Platform specialist**   | Hook strength, scroll-stop, engagement mechanics, CTA |
| **Jargon detector**       | Flag anything that smells like typical LinkedIn BS    |
| **Target reader: Role A** | Would your primary audience engage?                   |
| **Target reader: Role B** | Would your secondary audience follow the argument?    |

## Anti-patterns

Define what you DON'T want in your content. Examples:

- No triple-staccato statements (three short sentences pretending to be prose)
- No numbered listicles as structure
- No performative vulnerability
- No buzzwords or jargon from your industry

## Analytics Ingest Process

If your platform supports analytics export:

1. Export data periodically (monthly recommended)
2. Tell the agent to ingest from the file path
3. Agent updates content file frontmatter with metrics (impressions, engagements, metrics_updated)
