## welcome to the studio

We’re Sarica and Sam, a mom-and-pop online stationery shop based in Delaware, USA. Our shop is filled with products that we personally love to use, and we hope you enjoy them too! Our designs are made with thoughtfully curated licensed graphics, vintage art and ephemera, as well as exclusive original art by Sarica. Every order is packed with love and care in our home studio.
