## Frequently Asked Questions

What is Magic Patterns?

Magic Patterns is an AI prototype generator and AI prototyping tool that helps product teams go from idea to production, get user feedback, and build prototypes faster by generating UI.

How does Magic Patterns match your existing product UI?

Magic Patterns generates UI that matches your existing product. It can copy your app's styling and lets you import your design system so AI prototyping stays consistent with your components.

Can Magic Patterns be used as a prototype maker for rapid experiments?

Yes. Use Magic Patterns to prototype new features, iterate quickly, and test directly with customers — helping teams build an experimentation culture before committing to full build work.

How do teams collaborate in Magic Patterns?

It includes real-time multiplayer, so design and engineering can edit, share, and iterate live on prototypes together — speeding reviews and reducing hand-offs.

Is Magic Patterns a secure AI tool for prototyping?

Magic Patterns is enterprise-ready, secure, and compliant for AI prototyping. We are SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified, with reports available via our[Trust Center](https://trust.magicpatterns.com/).

What pricing options does Magic Patterns offer?

Pricing includes a free tier (limited) plus paid per-seat plans: Starter $20/seat/month, Business $100/seat/month, and Enterprise custom. Explore pricing at[magicpatterns.com/pricing](https://www.magicpatterns.com/pricing).
