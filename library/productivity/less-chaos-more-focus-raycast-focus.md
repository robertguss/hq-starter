## FAQs

You’ve got questions. We’ve got answers

#### Does Raycast Focus work in every browser?

Raycast Focus automatically blocks websites when using Safari, Chrome and Firefox (and other WebKit, Chromium and Firefox based browsers).

#### What happens if I need to access an app while in focus?

You can ask Raycast Focus to snooze blocking for a specific app or website. This will allow you to access the app or website while still blocking other distracting apps and websites. You can also pause the focus session to temporarily disable blocking.

#### How much does Raycast Focus cost?

Raycast Focus is free to use.
