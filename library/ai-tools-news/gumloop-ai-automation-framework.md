Building onboarding funnel query

The biggest drop-off is between Viewed Dashboard and Attempted Integration at 46%. This is the #1 problem area. Let me generate a visualization.

Building onboarding funnel chart

Here's where you're losing people:

**🚨 Biggest drop-off: Dashboard → Attempted Integration****46% of users** who view the dashboard never even try to connect an integration — that's 1,432 people falling off in a single step. This is your #1 problem.

**⚠️ Secondary drop-off: Attempted → Completed Integration** Of the users who do try, **37% fail to complete it.** Combined, only 22% of signups make it through the integration step at all.

| Step | Users | Drop-off |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Signed Up | 4,820 | — |
| Completed Profile | 3,940 | 18% |
| Viewed Dashboard | 3,105 | 21% |
| **Attempted Integration** | **1,673** | **⚠️ 46%** |
| **Completed Integration** | **1,056** | **⚠️ 37%** |
| Ran First Automation | 712 | 33% |
| Fully Onboarded | 481 | 32% |

Only **10% of signups** make it all the way through onboarding. The integration step is clearly the wall. Want me to break this down by acquisition channel, plan tier, or look at which specific integrations are failing most?

Does it depend where they came from?

![Image 53: Aron](https://www.gumloop.com/images/headshots/aron-headshot.png)

Gumloop

![Image 54: Querying channel funnel breakdown](https://www.gumloop.com/images/bigquery.png)

3 tool calls

The user wants to know if the onboarding drop-off depends on acquisition channel. Let me query channel-specific funnel data and create a comparison.

![Image 55: Querying channel funnel breakdown](https://www.gumloop.com/images/bigquery.png)

Checking customers table schema

![Image 56: Querying channel funnel breakdown](https://www.gumloop.com/images/bigquery.png)

Querying channel funnel breakdown

Building channel breakdown chart

Yes — channel makes a massive difference. Here's the breakdown:

**🟢 Referral is your best performer by far** 38% of referral signups fully onboard. They're the most motivated to connect an integration (70% attempt it) and complete it at the highest rate (87%).

**🟡 Organic is strong on completion, weak on attempt** Organic users who try an integration almost always finish (99% completion rate) — but only 48% ever attempt one.

**🔴 Paid Ads is a black hole** 1,540 signups — your biggest volume channel — but only **4.7% fully onboard**. Only 40% attempt an integration, and of those, barely half finish.

| Channel | Signups | Attempted | Completed | Onboarded | Conv % |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Referral | 710 | 70% | 87% | 270 | **38%** |
| Organic Search | 1,820 | 48% | 99% | 456 | 25% |
| Direct | 510 | 50% | 80% | 98 | 19% |
| Product Hunt / Social | 240 | 36% | 72% | 22 | 9% |
| **Paid Ads** | **1,540** | **40%** | **47%** | **72** | **⚠️ 5%** |

**The two clearest actions:**

1.   **Investigate why paid users abandon the integration step** — are they the wrong ICP, or is the onboarding experience failing them specifically?
2.   **Invest more in referral** — it converts at 8× the rate of paid. Even a modest referral incentive program could move the needle significantly.
