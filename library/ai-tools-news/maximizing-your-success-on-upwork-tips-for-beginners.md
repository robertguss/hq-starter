## Excerpt

In this video, I share essential tips for beginners on how to succeed on Upwork. I emphasize the importance of having a solid profile, putting in maximum effort, and consistently applying for jobs, ideally two to three proposals per day. I also recommend researching top-rated freelancers to understand what makes their profiles stand out. Additionally, I discuss the value of boosting proposals to increase visibility and the necessity of keeping all communications on Upwork for long-term success. I encourage you to take these steps seriously to build a strong foundation for your freelancing career.
