Ask Fusion to build|

### 1. Connect your code & context

Fusion plugs into your existing codebase and Figma files, so AI builds with your components, styles, and APIs.

### 2. Visually generate & edit

Watch AI build your UI in real-time, then jump in to visually edit when you need instant visual feedback or pixel-perfect control.

### 3. Ship to where your team works

Take your generated UI anywhere your team needs it: share live preview links, instantly create pull requests, or export clean code for local development.

### 4.Works within your workflow

Start builds where work happens: @mention Builder in Slack or assign Jira tickets, get progress updates as the agent works, then jump into Fusion to review and ship through your existing pipeline.
