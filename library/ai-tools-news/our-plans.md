## Frequently asked questions

You've got questions. We've got answers.

## Do you offer refunds?

Yes, if you are not satisfied with our product within the first 30 days, we will refund you.

## Student Discounts?

Yes, please email us at support@getrecall.ai from your student email address for a 20% student discount.

## What is Augmented Browsing?

Augmented Browsing is a feature in Recall that enhances your web experience by surfacing relevant insights from your personal knowledge base in real time. As you browse, Recall automatically detects keywords and concepts on the page and shows connections to content you've previously saved — helping you instantly recall past knowledge without interrupting your workflow. Since it's local-first, all processing happens on your device, ensuring privacy and security.

## What are AI summaries?

AI Summaries are generated using a mix of fine-tuned AI models and proprietary methods to generate high-quality concise or detailed summaries. The best way to create summaries is with our browser extensions on Chrome or Firefox.

## What content can you summarize?

Recall works best on YouTube Videos (up to 10 hours), PDFs (up to 300 pages), Articles, Google Docs, Blogs, and Recipes. We are quickly expanding the content we support.

## How do I cancel, change plans, or change credit cards?

You can manage your subscription from the "Manage Subscription" section found in the main app menu.

## What are unlimited personal notes?

You can use Recall as you would any other note-taking system. Simply create an unlimited amount of your own notes in Recall. Select an empty note from the menu in the Recall app. You can also generate quiz questions on your personal notes!

## What are AI quiz questions?

For all the cards you save into Recall, you can generate a quiz! This is part of our goal to enable long-term retention of content you care about. You can also fully customize these questions — adding more manually or changing the answers.

## What is Automatic categorization?

All AI summaries that you save into Recall have an automatic tag added to it which categorizes the card in the left panel of the application. This category is unique to you and existing tags you have.

## What is Automatic knowledge graph creation?

This is where the magic really happens! All the AI summaries that you save into Recall have keywords extracted and linked, enabling you to form connections to related content that you otherwise would have missed.

## What languages do you support?

You can translate AI summaries and your quiz questions to English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, and Russian.

## What does "Unlimited" mean in Recall's pricing?

Recall's Unlimited plan allows you to save unlimited notes and summaries. However, we reserve the right to pause, review, or adjust your subscription if we determine that your usage significantly exceeds reasonable levels. Please see our Fair Use Policy for details.

## Where can I find the full Terms of Service and Fair Use Policy?

You can read our full Terms of Service and Fair Use Policy on our website for details on usage, payments, and service policies.
