## What is LiteLLM?

LiteLLM simplifies **model access**, **spend tracking** and **fallbacks** across 100+ LLMs. Watch this demo, to learn more.

## Features

LiteLLM makes it easy for Platform teams to give developers LLM access

Spend Tracking

Budgets & Rate Limits

OpenAI-Compatible

LLM Fallbacks

Accurately charge teams for their usage.

*   [Attribute cost to key/user/team/org ->](https://docs.litellm.ai/docs/proxy/cost_tracking#getting-spend-reports---to-charge-other-teams-customers-users)

*   [Automatic spend tracking across OpenAI/Azure/Bedrock/GCP/etc. ->](https://github.com/BerriAI/litellm/blob/main/model_prices_and_context_window.json)

*   [Tag-based spend tracking ->](https://docs.litellm.ai/docs/proxy/enterprise#tracking-spend-for-custom-tags)

*   [Log spend to s3/gcs/etc. ->](https://docs.litellm.ai/docs/proxy/logging#s3-buckets)

*   [Prompt formatting support for HF models ->](https://docs.litellm.ai/docs/completion/prompt_formatting)

### Netflix uses LiteLLM to give developers Day 0 LLM access

![Image 46](https://framerusercontent.com/images/rsmzWRawhsqRlu3wFaXF4yGDf8.jpeg?width=800&height=800)

#### David Leen

Staff Software Engineer - Netflix

LiteLLM has let my team provide the latest LLM models to our users usually within a day of them being released. Without LiteLLM this would be hours of work each time a new model is announced. It means we don't have to transform inputs and outputs across providers and has saved us months of work.
