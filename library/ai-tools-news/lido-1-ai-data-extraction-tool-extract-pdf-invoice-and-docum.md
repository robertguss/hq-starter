## Frequently asked questions

What is Lido?
Lido is an AI-powered document processing platform that extracts structured data from any document and converts it into Excel, Google Sheets, or CSV. It works with PDFs, scans, images, and emails — no templates or manual data entry required. Lido supports invoices, receipts, bank statements, purchase orders, medical forms, and any other document type.

How does Lido extract data from documents?
Lido uses AI models to read and understand documents the way a human would — identifying fields, tables, and values regardless of format or layout. Upload a PDF, scan, or photo, and Lido returns structured spreadsheet data in seconds. No template setup or training is needed, even for documents it hasn’t seen before.

What types of documents can Lido process?
Lido processes any document type including invoices, receipts, bank statements, purchase orders, tax forms, medical claims, bills of lading, contracts, and handwritten documents. It handles PDFs, scanned images, photos, Word files, and email attachments in any language or currency.

How accurate is Lido’s data extraction?
Lido achieves 99%+ accuracy on structured documents like invoices and receipts. Its AI reads documents contextually rather than relying on fixed templates, so accuracy stays high even across different formats and layouts. Reference lists and custom validation rules can further improve accuracy for specialized document types.

Is Lido secure and HIPAA compliant?
Yes. Lido is SOC 2 Type 2 certified and HIPAA compliant. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Documents are automatically deleted after 23 hours, and Lido never uses customer data to train its AI models. Lido is trusted by healthcare organizations, financial services firms, and law firms handling sensitive data.

How much does Lido cost?
Lido offers a free trial with 50 pages and no credit card required. Paid plans start at $29 per month for 100 pages. The Scale plan is $7,000 per year for 42,000 pages and up to 10 users. Enterprise plans with custom volume start from $30,000 per year.

Do I need to create templates to use Lido?
No. Unlike traditional OCR tools, Lido requires zero template setup. Its AI understands document structure automatically, so it extracts the right fields from any format on the first upload. This means you can process documents from hundreds of different vendors or sources without configuring a single template.
