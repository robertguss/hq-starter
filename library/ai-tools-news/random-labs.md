## FAQ

### What makes Slate different from other coding agents?

Slate engineers it's own context so you don't have to. It has a built in compaction algorithm that surfaces the most important information at any time. This allows slate to always stay focused on the task at hand and avoid the [dumb zone](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rmvDxxNubIg).

### How do I plan without plan mode?

Slate plans implicitly. When given a task, slate will start by researching it, and then present it's plan to you. Slate doesn't have plan mode because a good plan is not made by asking 3 questions. A good plan is made by having an agent that has a discussion with you about how to solve a task.
