## Weeks of work. Overnight.

Hyperagent is the system of agents that does real work, learns how your organization operates, and deploys across your entire team.

01 - capabilities

## Not a chatbot. Powerful cloud agents with a full computing environment.

Hyperagent autonomously browses websites, pulls and analyzes data, generates images and video, creates interactive apps, and expertly uses your existing tools and services.

Hyperagent sourced, evaluated, and drafted personalized outreach for 27 AI engineering candidates.

- Extracted role requirements, must-have skills, seniority level, and team context from the internal job description.
- Opened a browser and searched LinkedIn, GitHub, Google Scholar, and personal sites to build a ranked candidate list with profiles, repos, and publications.
- Built a custom Talent Scout app with candidate cards, match scores, skills breakdowns, experience timelines, and side-by-side comparisons.
- Drafted personalized outreach emails for each candidate — referencing their specific work, projects, and research — ready to send in one click.

Hyperagent pulled overnight metrics, flagged urgent issues, and surfaced competitor moves — all compiled into a decision-ready morning brief by 7 AM.

- Connected to Shopify, HubSpot, and Gmail to pull overnight revenue, order volume, pipeline updates, and any flagged messages requiring attention.
- Scanned Slack channels and calendar to surface urgent threads, upcoming meetings, and scheduling conflicts for the day ahead.
- Searched the web for breaking competitor pricing changes, new product launches, and industry coverage mentioning the brand.
- Compiled everything into a Morning Brief with key metrics, prioritized actions with one-click responses, and a structured daily schedule — delivered to the inbox.

Hyperagent planned a full out-of-home campaign across LA — mapping available inventory against target accounts, scoring each placement by impressions, and mocking up creative on real street views.

- Pulled available OOH billboard and signage inventory across LA and plotted each location on an interactive map alongside target company HQ locations.
- Analyzed daily traffic volume and commute patterns for each placement to rank locations by expected impressions and proximity to target accounts.
- Opened a browser to capture Google Street View shots of the top-ranked placements from multiple angles.
- Overlaid creative onto each Street View image and compiled everything into a Campaign Planner app with map view, rankings, and budget optimizer.

02 - self improvement

## Learns the nuances of your business

Every session makes the next one better. Hyperagent generates new skills and memories worth retaining. Review manually or auto-accept, so agents silently accumulate knowledge without requiring explicit training.

### Teach it to evaluate deals like a top-tier investor.

The agent learns your 5-factor scoring rubric, technical evaluation framework, risk taxonomy. It produces due diligence reports in your format, with your weighting — finding the technical moats that generic analysis misses.

### Your brand's voice, style, and visual identity.

The agent internalizes your brand guidelines, color palette, photography style, and tone. Produces landing pages, Instagram Reels, campaign assets, and email sequences that are ready for market.

### Answers that reflect how your data actually works.

The agent codifies your metric definitions, schema mappings, and the business logic only your data team knows. Ask a question, get analysis grounded in your actual data model.

03 - deploy & orchestrate

## From agent to team infrastructure

Deploy agents everywhere your team already works. Then oversee your entire fleet — track costs, evaluate quality, and optimize with every run.

### Build your team

Save agents with a persistent configuration — system prompt, tool access, skills, memories, model selection, and budget limits — building the foundation for every run.

### Invocable by your org

Once you've created an agent, anyone can run it through Slack, via an MCP, a cron job, or wherever your team already works — along with all its skills, knowledge, and memory of previous sessions.

### Agent command center

Hyperagent gives you full oversight on every agent. Evaluate output quality with configurable rubrics. Track usage, cost, and A/B test performance across the fleet.
