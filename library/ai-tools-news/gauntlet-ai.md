Experienced engineers who want to learn how to fully integrate AI into their development process.

What will I learn?

In short, a lot. Initial lessons focus on system design for AI, building coding agents, prompt engineering, RAG pipelines, and deploying real-world AI applications. New material is added regularly.

We are building an AI-grader to give you feedback.

No, but if you’re serious about making the shift to AI-first development, check out our full-time training program. Experienced engineers can apply after getting started.
