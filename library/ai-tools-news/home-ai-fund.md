## Co-founding companies together **from idea to Impact**

**AI Fund is a venture studio.** We collaborate with entrepreneurs, venture funds, and corporate partners to create great companies that move humanity forward.

## Accelerating AI Innovation, **One startup at a Time**

[Meet The Team](https://aifund.ai/our-team/)
Founded by Andrew Ng, AI Fund is backed by over $370 million. As a venture studio, we work alongside entrepreneurs, venture funds, and corporate partners to build new companies rapidly and increase their odds of success. The studio is supported by top-tier VCs and partners including Sequoia Capital, NEA, Nikkei, and AES.

## Validate

We’ll surround you with a tiger team of business leaders and tech experts. Together, we’ll rapidly de-risk the idea.

## Build

With co-founder matching, pre-seed funding, and a hand-picked core team, we help launch your vision with momentum.

## Scale

Go fast. Grow fast. From fundraising to AI talent, we help you with everything you need to scale.

## We've partnered with extraordinary founders

We build AI businesses with entrepreneurs who know their industry inside and out. Deep industry knowledge paired with our AI expertise creates companies that tackle meaningful problems.

From making maritime shipping greener to empowering people in their relationships, to financial risk management, these collaborations turn industry challenges into thriving businesses. Each venture combines the unique strengths of our partners with our proven company-building methodology to create powerful new AI companies.
