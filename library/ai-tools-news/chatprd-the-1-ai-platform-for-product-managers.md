# Dashboard Redesign PRD

## Overview

Build a customizable analytics dashboard that helps product teams reduce time-to-insight and share actionable views across the organization. This redesign replaces the current static reporting interface with a drag-and-drop, widget-based experience.

## Goals & success metrics

*   Increase dashboard weekly active users by 25% within one quarter.
*   Cut setup time for a first dashboard from 12 minutes to under 4 minutes.
*   Raise internal NPS for reporting workflows from 31 to 45.

## Scope

Include drag-and-drop widgets, saved team layouts, and role-based visibility for high-value dashboards.

## User stories

*   As a product lead, I want to create a dashboard in under 4 minutes so that I can quickly share metrics.
*   As an analyst, I want to share saved views with specific teams without granting full edit access.
*   As an executive, I want role-based views that surface the KPIs most relevant to my function.

Applied edit

Added phased rollout plan: internal alpha → design partners → GA with KPI gates.

## Rollout plan

*   **Phase 1:** Internal alpha with core PM team (2 weeks)
*   **Phase 2:** Design partner rollout — 5 enterprise customers (4 weeks)
*   **Phase 3:** General availability with KPI gates for each milestone

Suggestion

Add explicit risk mitigation for migration of existing dashboard templates.

## Open questions

*   Data migration strategy for existing dashboard templates — backwards-compatible or clean break?
*   Performance benchmarks for real-time widget updates at scale (>50 concurrent viewers).
*   Accessibility requirements for drag-and-drop interactions on mobile and assistive devices.

Suggestion

Consider adding an “analytics template gallery” to reduce time-to-first-insight for new users.

## Technical requirements

*   Widget rendering latency < 200ms for charts with up to 10k data points.
*   Real-time collaboration via WebSocket with conflict-free operational transforms.
*   Export dashboards to PDF and PNG with pixel-perfect fidelity.
*   SSO and RBAC for enterprise deployment with audit logging.
