My name is Adam, and I'm the founder of Teamonics LLC and the creator of [@MindBranches](http://x.com/MindBranches) on 𝕏. Over the past few years, I've dedicated more than 2,000 hours to prompting the latest generative AI tools, creating thousands of MindBranch diagrams that have become a powerful resource for those looking to learn more about AI.

This journey began with a simple yet profound realization: while tools like ChatGPT excel at generating large amounts of information quickly, organizing and effectively applying that information can be challenging. MindBranches were created to address this need.

I use them as a way to break down and visually organize the outputs from large language models like ChatGPT into smaller, interconnected segments. These diagrams are not just visually appealing; they are practical, allowing anyone to understand complex concepts and large volumes of information at a glance.

The impact of MindBranches has been significant. In two years, I’ve grown an X account to over 100,000 followers, all drawn to the clear and useful insights these diagrams offer.

I wanted to share what I've learned about prompting with others, so I authored "[**The Art of Crafting Prompts**](https://mindbranches.gumroad.com/l/jnytho)," a book designed to help people quickly get the most out of generative AI. Within the book, I use MindBranches to simplify key prompting concepts, making it easier for readers to understand and apply what they learn. The book also contains a bonus section that teaches you how to make your own MindBranches.

Whether you're new to AI and want to jumpstart your prompting abilities or if you're interested in learning how to make your own MindBranches, you will get a ton of value out of, "[**The Art of Crafting Prompts**](https://mindbranches.gumroad.com/l/jnytho)."
