## From Spec To Shipped Code: Faster, With Less Drift.

A better way to build software with AI

### Spec-driven Development

Capture intent in clear, shareable Artifacts: PRDs, tech specs, and wireframes to align your team of humans and AI Agents.

![Image 4](https://framerusercontent.com/images/qNOtmJbWpUOAWFTI7F5oY6RE1tE.png?lossless=1&width=1744&height=1556)

![Image 5](https://framerusercontent.com/images/5EJsZuoOiIHbGEYQm2vQPqyQ84.png?lossless=1&width=1744&height=1556)

### Team Artifacts

Instantly share any Artifact with your teammates for quick feedback or real-time editing.

### Orchestrate Tasks

Break work into Epics, Tickets, and Phases. Decompose work to scale your engineering.

![Image 6](https://framerusercontent.com/images/Mc9IeDpQuUETthZ0Kv4LMfpjyGE.png?lossless=1&width=966&height=1321)

![Image 7](https://framerusercontent.com/images/oJTGYm1U73ssL0lUxb6EQgkHJk.png?lossless=1&width=1077&height=750)

### One Click Hand-Off

Pass full context to your favorite AI Agents like Cursor, Claude Code, Windsurf, etc., to generate code changes based on your specs.

### Review Changes

Traycer scans your codebase to verify every AI-generated change and apply course corrections so bad code never reaches production.

## Plan. Execute. Verify. No Surprises.

Traycer turns your intent into structured, editable specs, hands it to your favorite coding agent, and verifies the result, so you get production‑ready changes without surprises.

#### Faster Shipping On Complex Codebases

Break big work into phases with validation between steps.

![Image 9](blob:http://localhost/e3e0323e077ba186ab634b46d37f15b3)

#### Use The Agents You Already Love

Broad supported-agent list and the option to add more or use custom CLI agents.

#### Less Agent Drift, Fewer Hallucinations

Reduce drift and surprise by anchoring work to a structured plan.

#### Vibe Check While You Vibe Code

Verification generates comments categorized by severity.
