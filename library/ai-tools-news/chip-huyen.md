[![Image 1: Chip Huyen](https://huyenchip.com/assets/profile.jpeg)](https://www.instagram.com/huyenchip19/)

I’m Chip Huyen, a writer and computer scientist. I grew up chasing grasshoppers in a small rice-farming village in Vietnam.

My focus is on ML/AI systems in production. Previously, I worked on ML tooling at NVIDIA (core dev of [**NeMo**](https://github.com/NVIDIA/NeMo)), Snorkel AI, and Netflix. I also founded and sold an AI infra startup.

Running a startup taught me that being a founder is incredibly hard, so I try to support founders in any way I can. Some startups I’ve worked with included [Convai](https://convai.com/), [OctoAI](https://octo.ai/), and [Photoroom](https://www.photoroom.com/).

I graduated from Stanford, where I taught [ML Systems](https://cs329s.stanford.edu/). The lectures became the foundation for the book [**Designing Machine Learning Systems**](https://www.amazon.com/Designing-Machine-Learning-Systems-Production-Ready/dp/1098107969?&_encoding=UTF8&tag=chiphuyen-20&linkCode=ur2&linkId=0a1dbab0e76f5996e29e1a97d45f14a5&camp=1789&creative=9325), which is an Amazon #1 bestseller in AI and has been translated into [10+ languages](https://github.com/chiphuyen/dmls-book) (very proud)!

My new book **[AI Engineering](https://amzn.to/49j1cGS)** (2025) has been the most read book on the [O’Reilly](https://oreillymedia.pxf.io/c/5719111/2146021/15173) platform since its launch.

I spend a lot of time reading (instead of working). I like books that make me think and are also fun to read. I review books [@chipslib](https://instagram.com/chipslib) and on [Goodreads](https://www.goodreads.com/chiphuyen).

I’m active on [GitHub](https://github.com/chiphuyen). You can also find me on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=pwg2DGwAAAAJ&hl=en). I frequently speak at and occasionally host [events](https://huyenchip.com/events).

I enjoy learning about fun technical challenges and collaborating with great teams. [Reach out](https://huyenchip.com/communication) if you want to find a way to work together!
