## Excerpt

SamCart is an eCommerce checkout platform for online sellers and digital creators. SamCart's mission is to empower entrepreneurs to start or scale their online sales by easily selling more online, anywhere they interact with customers. SamCart is the alternative to the online store, focusing on deployable checkout experiences and not catalogs. Our goal is to (1) make it as quick and easy as possible for people to buy things and (2) boost our customers' conversions and average order value with every sale.
