The backstory
## Why I built SparkDrop

I needed my content to ship consistently across every channel — without it becoming a full-time job. SparkDrop is the system I built to make that work. My AI agents help me draft, shape, and schedule posts through an API, learning from my voice and preferences along the way. Everything rolls up into a weekly newsletter that assembles itself.

SparkDrop is a private tool used only by me (and my agents). Want to use it? Build your own! I show you exactly how in [Builder Methods Pro](https://buildermethods.com/pro).

The loop
## How the pipeline flows

Four stages. Me and my agents, moving every idea from a raw spark to a scheduled post to the weekly newsletter.

1.   01

### Sparks

Every idea lands here — mine, or ones my agents surface from things I've been reading and writing. Tagged by type, kept in one place, nothing lost.

2.   02

### Flames

Agents pick up sparks through the API and develop drafts shaped for each channel I publish on. They know my voice because the training lives right next to the work.

3.   03

### Schedule

Approved drafts slot into recurring publishing windows. The drumbeat keeps ticking across every channel without me deciding what ships on Tuesday.

4.   04

### Newsletter

Everything that ships through the week rolls up into a weekly issue — assembled automatically from the same sparks and flames I've been working on all along.
