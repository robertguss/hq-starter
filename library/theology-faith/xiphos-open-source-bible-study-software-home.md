**Xiphos** (Greek: ξίφος)

_a double-edged, single-hand sword 

 used by the ancient Greeks._

_"For the word of God is living and active, sharper than any two-edged sword, piercing to the division of soul and of spirit, of joints and of marrow, and discerning the thoughts and intentions of the heart."_ Hebrews 4:12 (ESV)

**Xiphos** is a Bible study tool written for Linux, UNIX, and Windows using [GTK](https://www.gtk.org/), offering a rich and featureful environment for reading, study, and research using modules from [The SWORD Project](https://www.crosswire.org/sword/) and elsewhere. It is open-source software, and available free-of-charge to all.
