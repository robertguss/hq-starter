# Celebrating 20 Years of Academic Excellence

Established in 2003, Shepherds Theological Seminary has been training and equipping servant-leaders for life and ministry for over 20 years. Our committment to this divine calling has not changed as we continue to offer the highest degrees available in ministry designed to continue impacting our world with godly men and women committed to sharing the gospel of Jesus Christ.

### DEGREES

![Image 8](https://shepherds.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/online-university-3.jpg)

#### Two Doctorates & Nine Masters

Shepherds is fully accredited and offers 11 full degree options along with several diploma and certificate programs, all designed to prepare you for life and ministry.

[View All Degree Programs](https://shepherds.edu/academics/#degrees)

### LOCATIONS

![Image 9](https://shepherds.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/STS-main-campus-web-1.jpg)

#### Choose Your Learning Site

From North Carolina to Texas and several church cluster sites, you have options to find the best location for you to pursue your seminary education and ministry training.

[Learn More About Each Site](https://shepherds.edu/about/#sites)

### WAYS TO LEARN

![Image 10](https://shepherds.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/STS-classroom-web.jpg)

#### In-Person or Online

Whether you prefer learning in person or online, Shepherds offers flexible options– including synchronous and asychronous–designed to fit your specific needs.

[Learn More and Apply](https://shepherds.edu/#enrollment)

## Options for Enrollment

Shepherds Theological Seminary has built its academic programs to prepare students for real life and real ministry. Our curriculum has been thoughtfully designed to be rigorous, relevant, and rewarding. Whether you want the in-person campus experience, or the convenience of in-home study, or a little of both, STS has you covered. Be sure to explore our degrees and find the option that works best for you!
