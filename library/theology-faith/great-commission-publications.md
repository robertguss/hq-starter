### FAQ

Do you sell internationally?

Yes, absolutely! Contact us directly for a quote on shipping and to find out other options for getting our Christ-centered materials.

How do I find my previous orders?

We are happy to send your previous orders for your reference. Feel free to call or email us. All orders placed after December 2024 will appear in your Account under Orders after you log in. Any new orders will appear there as well. Thank you for your patience on our new website.

Do you offer training?

Yes! We have free on-demand training [here.](https://vimeo.com/channels/gcptraining "GCP free video training") Share with your volunteers so they can learn on their own time. We also offer regular webinars. Sign up for our emails so you never miss a thing! You can also schedule a one-on-one consultation or on-site training by reaching out to us.

How do I access my digital materials?

When you purchase items with digital downloads, you will receive a link through email. If you're having trouble, feel free to reach out!
