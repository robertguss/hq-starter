Dr. K. Scott Oliphint speaks about the doctrine of God and his book *[God with Us: Divine Condescension and the Attributes of God](http://www.wtsbooks.com/product-exec/product_id/8055/nm/God+with+Us%3A+Divine+Condescension+and+the+Attributes+of+God+%28Paperback%29?utm_source=reformedforum&utm_medium=blogpartners)*. Dr. Oliphint is Professor of Apologetics and Systematic Theology at [Westminster Theological Seminary](http://www.wts.edu) in Glenside, PA. In this episode, he entertains several general questions about the doctrine of God before moving to a more pointed discussion of how an infinite, eternal, and immutable God can create and then relate to that creation.

Participants: [Camden Bucey](https://reformedforum.org/people/camden-bucey/),[Jared Oliphint](https://reformedforum.org/people/jared-oliphint/),[Jonathan Brack](https://reformedforum.org/people/jonathan-brack/),[K. Scott Oliphint](https://reformedforum.org/people/k-scott-oliphint/)
