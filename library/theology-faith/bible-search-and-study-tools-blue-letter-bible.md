**Parallel Version Comparison on the Web**

For over a decade, Blue Letter Bible has offered a unique feature on our mobile apps: a parallel Bible version comparison where you can read two translations side by side. This gives users a quick, effective way to perform word and phrase studies. It also allows users to readily compare original language texts to the English translations.

We have received countless requests to bring this feature to the website over the years, and now, it's finally here. We are beyond excited to announce that we have added this functionality to our website! The study materials from _The Scofield Reference Bible_ are now available on Blue Letter Bible. This extensive work, published in 1909, was born out of C.I. Scofield's conviction that printed Bibles were lacking thorough, in-line study resources. In the introduction, (which you can read [here](https://www.blueletterbible.org/scofield-reference-bible/references/introduction)), the "distinctive features" of this work are clearly outlined. From extensive inline notes on every verse of the Bible to a detailed alphabetical index of every topic discussed within the resource, this work is comprehensive in its approach to examining Scripture. To learn more about what is offered, check out our [detailed blog post](https://blogs.blueletterbible.org/blb/2026/02/10/scofield-reference-bible/).
