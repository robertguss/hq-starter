I’m Sean Mann - a guitarist, course creator, video creator, and lifelong blues obsessive.

I’ve spent the last 17 years chasing tone, while striving to emulate the flow and passion from my guitar heroes . My style is rooted in players like **Stevie Ray Vaughan**, **Hendrix**, **Mayer**, **Slash** and all the greats who say more with one note than most do with fifty.

These courses are everything I wish I had when I was stuck in the boxes - obsessing over the right hand attack, wondering how to make licks sound like _me_. They’re built to take you from “just playing notes” to **playing with purpose**, **groove**, and **conviction**.

Whether you’re looking to clean up your technique or unlock creativity across the neck, I made these courses to help you finally sound like the guitarist you’ve always heard in your head.

Lets dive in and make some _real_ music.
