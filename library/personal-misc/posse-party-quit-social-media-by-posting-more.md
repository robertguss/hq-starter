### Everyone's invited!

Join the [POSSE](https://indieweb.org/POSSE): post to your site, and let it crosspost wherever your audience follows. Ghost the algorithms without becoming one yourself.

### It's a ME party…

This isn't another SaaS subscription—you [host it yourself](https://github.com/searlsco/posse_party/blob/main/DEPLOY.md)! You can even invite friends to syndicate their own sites from your instance.

### …and a FREE party 🎉

Total cost to you: $0. Built for individuals who love creating but hate wasting time doom-scrolling. [Free for non-commercial use](https://github.com/searlsco/posse_party/?tab=readme-ov-file#license).
