Questions?
## Frequently asked  questions

Everything you need to know about SEO Utils features, pricing, and data privacy.

Can SEO Utils track rankings on AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT?

Yes! SEO Utils includes an LLM Rank Tracker that monitors your brand visibility in AI search results including Google AI Overview, AI Mode, and ChatGPT (with and without web search). Perfect for Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). [Learn more](https://help.seoutils.app/guide/llm-rank-tracker).

What is the source of backlinks and traffic data?

SEO Utils pulls backlinks and traffic data from your DataForSEO account, and you need a DataForSEO account to pull backlink, traffic, keyword data (Just like OpenAI API). Read more about it [here](https://help.seoutils.app/guide/seo-data-source).

How many keywords can I run clustering on?

Unlimited keywords with no extra cost or credit-based system. SEO Utils supports both SERP Clustering (up to 50K-200K keywords) and Semantic Keyword Clustering with multiple pre-trained language models.

Does SEO Utils integrate with Google Search Console?

Yes! SEO Utils offers extended data retention beyond 16 months, multi-account management, natural language filtering, and features like bulk mention checking and keyword cannibalization detection. [Learn more](https://help.seoutils.app/guide/google-search-console).

Can I generate white-labeled reports for clients?

Absolutely! Create shareable, customized SEO reports with your company logo and branding. You can create password-protected report links and even embed reports on websites or dashboards. [Learn more](https://help.seoutils.app/guide/white-labeled-client-report).

Which languages does the keyword clustering tool support?

Many languages. It depends on which embedding model you select. Read more about it [here](https://help.seoutils.app/guide/semantic-keyword-clustering).

Does the keyword clustering tool require a DataForSEO account?

No, it runs on your computer without any dependencies.

Do I need SEO Utils if I already have an Ahrefs or SEMRush account?

Why not? With more data comes greater insights. SEO Utils offers unique features like unlimited keyword clustering, LLM rank tracking, AI-powered metadata optimization, and local SEO tools that complement traditional SEO platforms.

What if I don't want to pay the commitment on the Backlinks API of DataForSEO?

SEO Utils has [a feature](https://help.seoutils.app/guide/rent-dataforseo-api-key) that will allow you to rent a key from SEO Utils without having to make a commitment to access Backlinks API. Please note that you don't need to pay a monthly commitment for keyword or traffic data.

What does "One year of updates" mean?

One license key allows you to use the current app version forever. They are also eligible for one year of free updates to the app. If you don't want to renew the license after one year, you can continue to use the last version of the app that you have access to.

How does SEO Utils handle data privacy and storage?

Your data remains completely secure on your local machine. SEO Utils is a desktop application that stores everything locally—we never collect, access, or transmit your website data to our servers or any third parties.
