## The Power

## of Open Source

Twenty is GPL-licensed. It means you own, not rent, the software. You get the simplicity of SaaS, with the freedom and cost savings of Open Source.

### Thriving community

Hundreds of contributors came together to build this software, creating a welcoming community that freely shares its expertise and offers support.

### Open Roadmap

Missing a feature?

Start a discussion, report an issue, contribute the code, or even fork the repository.

### Secured by design

Benefit from the collective scrutiny of a global community. This allows the rapid identification and rectification of vulnerabilities, ensuring a secure environment for your data.
