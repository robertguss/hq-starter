## What is a Mini Book?

> Short, actionable books that can be consumed quickly in order to serve a specific person with a specific problem.

As you can see the most important part of a mini book is that it’s actionable and can be consumed quickly to serve a person with a specific problem.

If we use authors, can do this well then it doesn’t matter how long the book is. Most of all people will come back and buy more of your mini books to solve more of their problems.

I believe that mini books look different when it comes to publishing on Amazon KDP and publishing to sell directly.

Here’s what I mean:

*   Amazon: can be more traditionally formatted and up to 100 pages.

*   Direct: can be formatted for more white space and up to 50 pages.
