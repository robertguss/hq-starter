This article will explain how to access your virtual machine's (VM) configuration.

[](https://kb.parallels.com/117287)
## VM is not running:

1.   Start Parallels Desktop
2.   Click on the Parallels icon on the Mac menu bar and choose **Control Center**.

[![Image 29](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/u9xdNJFtkQ8.jpg)](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/u9xdNJFtkQ8.jpg)
3.   Click on the cogwheel.

[![Image 30](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/Screenshot%20at%20Apr%2006%2013-51-29.png)](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/Screenshot%20at%20Apr%2006%2013-51-29.png)

[](https://kb.parallels.com/117287)
## VM is in Windowed mode (running or not):

1.   Click on the Parallels icon on the Mac menu bar and choose **Configure** under your virtual machine section.

[![Image 31](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/nyd1QTWnyjw.jpg)](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/nyd1QTWnyjw.jpg)
2.   OR Click on the cogwheel in the top right corner of the virtual machine's window.

[![Image 32](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/Screenshot%20at%20Apr%2006%2013-54-43.png)](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/Screenshot%20at%20Apr%2006%2013-54-43.png)

[](https://kb.parallels.com/117287)
## VM is running in Coherence/Window mode:

1.   Click on the Parallels icon on the Mac menu bar and choose **Configure**.

[![Image 33](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/c0MS48lEW4Y.jpg)](https://kb.parallels.com/Attachments/kcs-7292/c0MS48lEW4Y.jpg)
