## Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most popular questions about ONLYOFFICE

Is ONLYOFFICE really free?

Yes, it is. ONLYOFFICE is a free and open-source office suite distributed under the AGPL 3.0 license. The source code repositories are available on [GitHub](https://github.com/ONLYOFFICE).

Besides, we offer a range of free solutions that you can choose from based on your needs. These include absolutely [free desktop editors](https://www.onlyoffice.com/desktop) for Windows/Linux/macOS, [free mobile apps](https://www.onlyoffice.com/download-desktop#mobile) for iOS/Android, [the free Startup plan](https://www.onlyoffice.com/docspace-registration) of ONLYOFFICE DocSpace Cloud.

Free community builds of ONLYOFFICE solutions for self-hosting can be found [here](https://www.onlyoffice.com/download-community).

What is ONLYOFFICE used for?

ONLYOFFICE provides [a full range of tools](https://www.onlyoffice.com/office-suite) to create, edit and collaborate on text documents, spreadsheets, presentations, PDF forms and regular PDF files on web, desktop and mobile platforms.

You can use the online editors [within any business platform](https://www.onlyoffice.com/all-connectors) or opt for the room-based content collaboration platform [ONLYOFFICE DocSpace](https://www.onlyoffice.com/docspace) which already comes with the integrated editors. Besides, you are able to [embed](https://www.onlyoffice.com/developer-edition) the editors into your own web app.

[Desktop editors](https://www.onlyoffice.com/desktop) for working with office documents offline, as well as [mobile apps](https://www.onlyoffice.com/download-desktop#mobile) are also available.

Is ONLYOFFICE safe to use?

Yes, it is. We [prioritize security](https://www.onlyoffice.com/security) with robust measures. ONLYOFFICE is open-source and GDPR compliant – which means it fits all the legal criteria for data protection. ONLYOFFICE includes file encryption and multiple options for secure sharing and access control. Besides, our solutions can be deployed on private servers.

Can I use ONLYOFFICE offline?

Yes, you can. To do so, download the free [ONLYOFFICE Desktop Editors](https://www.onlyoffice.com/download-desktop#desktop) which is available for Windows, Linux, and macOS.

Does ONLYOFFICE have ads?

No, ONLYOFFICE is ad-free. We don't incorporate any external ads into our solutions.

Who owns ONLYOFFICE?

ONLYOFFICE is owned by Ascensio System SIA, a Latvian company which is a subsidiary of OnlyOffice Capital Group Pte. Ltd. The beneficiaries of OnlyOffice Capital Group Pte. Ltd. are listed in the registry of Singapore companies by ACRA. The business profile is available [here](https://onlyo.co/3UKteVe).

Who is the CEO of ONLYOFFICE?

The founder and the CEO of ONLYOFFICE is [Lev Bannov](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lev-bannov-6193aa32/) (Istanbul, Turkey).
