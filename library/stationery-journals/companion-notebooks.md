# Companion Notebooks

Already have a daily or weekly planner that you use and love? We get it! No need to give it up. We created our quarterly goal-setting companion notebooks to bring our entire MāksëLife system (minus the weekly planning spreads and coaching tips) to your existing planner. The fan-favorite Compass Assessment is included, along with intention-setting, annual and monthly goal-setting, monthly reflections (an essential part of our system!), and other tools to set your vision and take action toward your goals. Just slip a new one into your planner pocket or notebook folio each quarter so your dreams—and the path to get there—are never far away. They’re undated so you can start using them anytime, but this [dated monthly sticker kit](https://makselife.com/collections/shop/products/dated-sticker-kit) will keep things current. And to add Sierra’s inspirational coaching tips to your planning pages, pick up our [weekly coaching tip stickers](https://makselife.com/collections/shop/products/coaching-sticker-pack).

Need extra writing space? Our [daily sticky notepad](https://makselife.com/collections/shop/products/daily-sticky-notepad) lets you add a full day of planning wherever you need it. And our A5 [dot-grid notebooks](https://makselife.com/collections/shop/products/teal-dot-grid-notebooks) (which also fit in your planner pocket or our notebook folios) are perfect for jotting down lists, notes, and bursts of inspiration. For planning on the go, add this [expandable pen pouch](https://makselife.com/collections/shop/products/expandable-pen-pouch) to keep your pens and highlighters protected in transit, then ready to write when it’s time to plan.
