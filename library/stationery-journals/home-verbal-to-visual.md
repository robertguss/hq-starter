## A Decade of Teaching, Distilled Into One Comprehensive Curriculum

I spent a decade teaching visual thinking through workshops and courses, learning what works and what doesn't. This curriculum brings together all of those insights into a single, cohesive learning journey. No more hunting through scattered resources—everything you need is here, organized to support your growth from day one.

This curriculum teaches you how to sketch out your own visual models for use as a **learning**, **problem-solving**, and **communication** tool.

![Image 7](https://verbaltovisual.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/CurriculumLessons.jpeg)

Yes, you learn the basics of diagramming and drawing, but more importantly, you learn how to direct your visualization efforts toward a cohesive model that becomes a visual asset you can use for years to come.

## The Visual Thinking Journey: Four Comprehensive Modules

Within this curriculum you'll find a complete system for developing your visual thinking skills in a way that will serve you for years to come.

* * *

#### **Part 1: Foundations**

![Image 8](https://verbaltovisual.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/CurriculumModules.001.jpeg)

Start with the right mindset and approach. Learn what visual thinking really is, discover the three key shifts that unlock lasting benefits, and overcome common barriers as you develop your personal visual thinking roadmap. **Watch the first lesson below**.

#### **Part 2: Methods**

![Image 9](https://verbaltovisual.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/CurriculumModules.002.jpeg)

Master the specific techniques that bring ideas to life. I share a flexible framework for creating powerful visual models and you'll build your mark-making skills from the ground up. These aren't art lessons—they're thinking tools.

#### **Part 3: Reflections**

![Image 10](https://verbaltovisual.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/CurriculumModules.003.jpeg)

Develop the skills to evaluate and improve your work over time. Learn what makes good models effective, discover guiding principles for visual thinking, and learn how to keep things fresh as you keep growing your skills.

#### **Part 4: Examples**

![Image 11](https://verbaltovisual.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/CurriculumModules.004.jpeg)

Get inspired by real visual thinking work from practitioners around the world. Study frameworks from experts like Dave Gray and Abby Covert, explore creative approaches from visual storytellers, and see how others within the community are using these skills in their professional and personal lives.
