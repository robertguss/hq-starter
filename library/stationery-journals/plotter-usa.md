STEP 1

What is PLOTTER

* * *

PLOTTER is a new analog system that will make daily creative work enjoyable for creators, conceptors, entrepreneurs, leaders, researchers, makers, and everyone else who brings the world something new. PLOTTER creates tools that draw on a wealth of materials, technology, and craftsmanship to help you realize your ideas and imagination.

![Image 31](https://plotterusa.com/cdn/shop/files/HowToUsePLOTTER_ill_W1440H700_4C_202501.jpg?v=1768519722&width=1440)

STEP 2

Creativity without interruption

* * *

PLOTTER defies conventional inconveniences of ring systems by maximizing the use of space, increasing portability, simplifying structure, and freeing up your time to focus on what’s important–your valuable ideas. We designed a system that helps you create effortlessly, organize efficiently, archive, and re-ignite ideas seamlessly.

![Image 32: A person holding a pen and writing the word PLOTTER on a ring leather binder.](https://plotterusa.com/cdn/shop/files/Banner-1440x700-PersonandPLOTTER_0.jpg?v=1748974354&width=1440)

STEP 3

You are PLOTTER

* * *

We also define “PLOTTER” as someone who uses their creativity to clear a path to a new future. A PLOTTER plans, organizes, and creates. PLOTTER gives you simple, intuitive, and long-lasting tools to expand your creativity, bring new ideas, and envision the future.
