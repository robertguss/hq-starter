## Whiteboard.

MyScript Notes offers four note formats — notebooks, documents, boards, and PDFs — each tailored to different needs and note-taking styles.

### Notebook

Create unlimited notebooks with fixed-size pages for your daily notes, and personalize covers to stay organized. You can also customize pages by choosing the size, orientation, paper color, and pattern that work best for you.

![Image 19: A ipad with a nebo app screen](https://www.myscript.com/_ipx/f_webp&q_80&s_706x488/notes/hero/notebook-hero-img.png)

![Image 20: A ipad with a nebo app screen](https://www.myscript.com/_ipx/f_webp&q_80&s_706x488/notes/hero/board-hero-img.png)

### Board

Designed for visual note-taking and quick ideas, the board is an expandable canvas where you can focus on your thoughts. Enjoy a freeform digital whiteboard that lets you create freely, with write-to-text and shape conversion built in.

### Document

The document helps you structure content that adapts automatically as you change the layout or screen orientation. Edit with pen gestures, add titles, checklists, math, and diagrams — then turn it all into clean text and perfect shapes, ready to share.

![Image 21: A ipad with a nebo app screen](https://www.myscript.com/_ipx/f_webp&q_80&s_706x488/notes/hero/document-hero-img.png)

### PDF

Import your files into MyScript Notes as multi-page PDFs, so everything stays in one place — perfectly organized. Jump from page to page, review and annotate freely with your pen — your notes stay editable and searchable.

Back up your entire library of notes in one go, for maximum security and peace of mind.
