## Enjoy a control panel designed to **adapt to you**

### Customize Everything

Feel right at home with an admin area that adapts to your users.

![Image 8: An intuitive control panel with various customizations like different collections such as Galleries, Pages, and Blog.](https://statamic.com/img/content/screenshots/home-cp-v6.webp)

### Powerful Blueprints

Organize fields to structure your content, forms, and other data types.

![Image 9: a Blueprint area showing the organization of fields. Fields are at different widths and different data types.](https://statamic.com/img/content/screenshots/home-blueprints-v6.webp)

### 40+ Fieldtypes

Always the right interface for the job, from block editors to toggle switches.

### Block-based Editing

Bard is an infinitely powerful block-based editor, with fly-out previews.

![Image 10: Sets offer a powerful block-based editing experience, with fly-out previews. A 'search set' panel is shown, with a fly-out preview of a newsletter.](https://statamic.com/img/content/screenshots/home-cp-blocks.webp)

### Live Preview

See your content changes come to life without ever leaving the control panel.

![Image 11: Live Preview in action, showing a page with a live preview of the content. The live preview is an interesting type-based design with a large heading.](https://statamic.com/img/content/screenshots/home-live-preview-v6.webp)

### Multi-Site

Build sites that span multiple languages, domains, or sub-brands.

![Image 12: Multi-Site in action, showing a dropdown menu for the different locales. Some are not yet active, others are active and have a green dot next to them.](https://statamic.com/img/content/screenshots/home-multisite-v6.webp)
