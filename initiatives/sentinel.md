## Goal

Ship Sentinel (ad blocker) to the App Store.

## Context

Sentinel is a premium iOS ad blocker built around Apple's new `NEURLFilter` API on iOS 26+. Its core differentiation is simple and concrete: it blocks ads and trackers system-wide without occupying the device's VPN slot, so users can keep a real VPN active at the same time. The product is aimed at privacy-first iPhone users who currently have to choose between blocking and VPN protection because existing solutions are either Safari-only or VPN-based.

The strategic opportunity is being early on a new Apple API with a clear positioning wedge. Existing competitors such as 1Blocker, AdGuard, and Wipr either stop at browser blocking or take over the system VPN slot. Sentinel is intended to be the polished first mover for users who want system-wide blocking plus real VPN coexistence.

## Key Results

- [ ] A v1 PRD is completed with clear scope, positioning, monetization, and launch assumptions
- [ ] The NEURLFilter-based technical approach is validated well enough to support a credible MVP plan
- [ ] Sentinel's differentiation is captured in a clear launch message: system-wide blocking that does not steal the VPN slot
