## Goal

Complete and ship Sweep to the App Store.

## Context

Sweep is an iOS 26-only photo cleaner positioned as the honest alternative to predatory subscription apps. It solves a common consumer problem — duplicate photos, screenshots, oversized videos, and photo-library clutter — without the usual weekly billing traps, analytics SDKs, or stale multi-iOS UI compromises. The product strategy is to pair a premium native feel with a one-time purchase model and strict privacy posture.

The wedge is both product and positioning. Sweep's core product strengths are instant "Heavy Hitters," duplicate and quality cleanup, video compression, Live Photo conversion, semantic search on capable devices, and cleanup history with undo. Its market strengths are lifetime pricing, Liquid Glass-native design, and zero third-party analytics. The app is being built in parallel with a waitlist campaign at `sweep.app`.

- [ ] Sweep ships to the App Store by 2026-07-07 with the agreed MVP scope intact
- [ ] The MVP includes Heavy Hitters, duplicate/similar cleanup, quality cleanup, video compression, Live Photo conversion, semantic search on supported devices, and cleanup history + undo
