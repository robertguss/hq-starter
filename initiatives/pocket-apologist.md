## Goal

Ship Pocket Apologist to the App Store.

## Context

Pocket Apologist is an iOS app designed to help Christians practice and defend their faith through AI-powered conversation roleplay, instant objection handling, and daily skill-building. It is aimed at believers who know apologetics matters but freeze in real conversations, especially young adults, group leaders, and students facing live objections. The product strategy is to build confidence through guided practice rather than simply shipping a static content library.

The market wedge is strong: apologetics + evangelism + AI is still largely unclaimed, while adjacent faith apps have already proven substantial demand for AI-assisted religious products. Pocket Apologist's differentiation is the combination of curated theological content, AI roleplay and coaching, Duolingo-style progression, and theological guardrails. Robert's own conversion story is a load-bearing part of the product's foundation even when it stays beneath the surface of the messaging.

## Key Results

- [ ] The MVP scope is clearly defined around quick answers, AI conversation coach, daily training, conversation toolkit, topic library, and conversation journal
